Frequently Asked Questions

The Army Combat Fitness Test (ACFT) is the U.S. Army's official physical fitness assessment. It consists of six events that measure strength, power, speed, agility, and endurance. The test is crucial for military readiness and career progression, as it evaluates a soldier's ability to perform combat-related tasks.

Our ACFT Calculator uses the official Army scoring standards and algorithms to provide highly accurate results. The calculator is regularly updated to reflect the latest Army standards and scoring tables, ensuring you get the most precise assessment of your performance.

A passing ACFT score requires a minimum of 60 points in each event, with a total minimum score of 360 points. However, some Army positions may require higher minimum scores. Our calculator helps you track your progress and determine if you meet these requirements.
