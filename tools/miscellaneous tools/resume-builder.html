A good resume template should be clean, professional, and ATS-friendly. It should have clear section headings, consistent formatting, and enough white space for readability. Our templates are designed with these principles in mind and are optimized for both human readers and ATS systems.

To make your resume ATS-friendly, use standard section headings, avoid complex formatting, include relevant keywords from the job description, and use a simple, clean layout.
