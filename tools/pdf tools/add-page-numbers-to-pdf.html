About This Tool How to Use Description

📄 Add Page Numbers to PDF is a free online tool that allows you to quickly and easily add customizable page numbers to your PDF documents. Perfect for academic papers, reports, technical manuals, and any professional document that requires pagination.

✨ This tool provides various customization options including number position, format, font styling, and the ability to add prefixes/suffixes. Our tool processes your files locally in your browser, ensuring complete privacy and security.

🚀 Whether you need simple page numbers or advanced formatting like "Page X of Y", our tool handles it all with ease and precision, saving you valuable time.

  1. 📤 Upload your PDF file - Drag and drop your file into the upload area or click the browse button to select a file from your device.
  2. ⚙️ Configure page numbering options - Choose where to place the numbers, format (1,2,3 or i,ii,iii or A,B,C), starting page, font size, color, and style. Add optional prefix/suffix text like "Page" or "of total".
  3. 👁️ Preview the result - See how your page numbers will look with the settings you've chosen.
  4. ✅ Process your PDF - Click the "Add Page Numbers" button to apply the numbering to your document.
  5. 💾 Download your numbered PDF - Save the processed file to your device when complete.

Our Add Page Numbers to PDF tool offers a comprehensive solution for adding professional pagination to any PDF document. This free online utility processes your files securely in your browser without uploading them to any server.
