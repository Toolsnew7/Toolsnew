About This Tool How to Use Description

PDF Comparison Tool is a powerful free utility that allows you to identify all differences between two PDF documents. Our advanced comparison algorithm analyzes text content, images, formatting, and layout to highlight even the smallest changes. This is perfect for legal professionals reviewing contracts, academics comparing research papers, or anyone who needs to track changes between document versions. Unlike other tools, our PDF comparator works entirely in your browser, ensuring your sensitive documents remain private and secure.

  1. Upload your first PDF document by clicking on the "PDF Document 1" box or drag and drop your file directly into it.
  2. Similarly, upload your second PDF document into the "PDF Document 2" box.
  3. Select your desired comparison type: text comparison, visual comparison, or highlight differences.
  4. For more precise results, you can adjust the advanced options like sensitivity level, highlight color, and comparison mode.
  5. Click the "Compare PDFs" button to start the comparison process.
  6. Review the results showing differences highlighted in a side-by-side view and in the detailed differences table below.
  7. You can download a comprehensive comparison report by clicking "Download Comparison Report".

Our PDF Comparison Tool is an advanced free online utility designed to efficiently detect and highlight differences between two PDF documents.
